Features

Everything you need to track crypto

Built natively for macOS with performance and simplicity in mind.

⚡

Real-time WebSocket Prices

Live price streaming directly from Binance WebSocket API. No polling, no delays — instant updates as trades happen.

📊

30 Top Cryptocurrencies

Track the top 30 coins including BTC, ETH, SOL, BNB, XRP, ADA, DOT, LINK, AVAX, and many more.

🎮

Multi-select Status Bar

Choose up to 10 cryptocurrencies to display simultaneously in your menu bar with live prices and 24h change.

💰

Smart Price Formatting

Intelligent decimal precision — 4 decimals for small coins, 1 decimal for high-value assets, with comma separators.

🔄

Auto-Reconnection

Robust connection handling with automatic reconnect on network failures. Your prices always stay up to date.

⚡

Lightweight & Native

Built with Swift and Cocoa. No Electron, no bloat. Runs as a menu bar app with minimal memory and CPU usage.
